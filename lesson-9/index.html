About Klimagator

Klimagator started by a group of Information Technology students from Brigham Young University who aims to make the community a better and a safer place. Being aware of how the weather affects the lives of the people, Klimagator brings the up-to-date forecasting that anyone could easily access to whether it is a phone or personal computer.

Klimagator delivers accurate weather forecasting wired through Geocoding location information from any device. It provides details of a 24/7 weather forecast, weather data, current technology, specific location access, and actual observation for any location determined by a search string to maximize the standard of living and productivity. KlimaGator delivers first-rate information to promote safety and awareness within the community. We deliver timely warnings to prevent and mitigate the effects of natural disasters and suggest safety travel/transportation schemes. We serve people with options to recognize the impact of weather changes and make the best resolutions.
