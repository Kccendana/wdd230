Fish Haven Christmas Tree Lighting Set for Dec 1st

Organizers announce details for the annual event, including gingerbread house decorating, gift-wrapping contest, amusement rides, food trucks, and more.

The annual lighting of the Christmas tree on Fish Haven for the first time in two years of pandemic and isolation will illuminate the venue this year on the first of December. With 5 million twinkling lights, highlighting the largest light displays in the city. On the Main Road, the entire section will be closed for the events from 5 p.m. The grand stage presents Idaho personalities with Aaron Paul as the special guest.

In addition to rides and holiday shopping, Blu Ice Gardens features "The Winter Escapade," an ice-skating show, a Santa's workshop, and a Christmas Town Express train. Thrill-seekers can jump on the Tuyokern Flyer pendulum ride, giving a Reindeer-worthy view of the 5 million lights below. Come and ride with us!!

Besides, the famed Mistletoe Spree will be at the heart of the grand illumination. The spree will feature the new Winter Parade and lantern display. Also, the rides on Ikotpamor Polar Express loop around the festivities. There will be a showcase of different holiday wreaths, soaps, candles for presents, cakes, winter cookies, homemade jams and jellies, and many more that will satisfy your Christmas present cravings and celebration.

At 6 p.m., Mayor John Anderson will acknowledge and impose the awards of the sought-after cooking trophies of the top three champions of his Christmas recipe gem state campaign. Also, he will announce the grand winners of the winter float garden for the first time. The Anchor will then welcome Santa and Mrs. Claus and their elves to the stage for a full-hour merriment show.

At the finale, the Fish Haven has chosen Minico High School to accompany the Mayor and Santa for the official lighting of the Fish Haven Christmas tree. The 30-foot jumbo Christmas tree, a fresh cut evergreen, will be filled and decorated with chandelier or teardrop ornaments, glass balls, bright-colored tinsels and garlands, an assortment of colored lights, and the traditional tree toppers as a part of Christmas festivity. The Christmas tree stands tall over multiple giant mushrooms adorned with led Christmas lights.

The photo booths will spread across the venue, and food chains will satisfy all revelers on sight. The lighting will go on the following day, whenever postponed.
